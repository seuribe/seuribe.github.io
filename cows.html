The Cow Book


The Cow Book was started in January 2022 and was finished (or was it?) on May the same year. It was a creative outlet, an excercise in themes, form and technique using a fixed set of elements (cows, mountains, clouds, trees), and an escape from stressful days during a three month stay in Rotterdam.
