Workshops

Part of my work involves organizing and running workshops.
I also enjoy creating playful activities to make difficult topics engaging and easier to discuss.
Some of the topics I love to discuss are Post- & Neocolonialism, the Climate Crisis, sustainable production and consumption, and techno-utopianism.

I've trained as a referent for Global Learning with Bildung Trifft Entwicklung and have led workshops using EN-Roads as an Ambassador from Climate Interactive.

I've also created and led workshops on Artificial Intelligence for schools with IMAGINARY.

Feel free to contact me to learn more about the workshops.
